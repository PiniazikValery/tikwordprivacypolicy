Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how our English language learning application collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

The application may collect and store the following information:

  • Learning Data: words you add for learning, your progress, and training statistics
  • App Settings: your selected native language and other user preferences
  • Usage Data: information about how you interact with the app to improve the learning experience

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used to:

  • Personalize your learning experience and prioritize words for training sessions
  • Track your language learning progress
  • Provide AI-powered explanations and suggest new words to learn
  • Improve app functionality and quality

3. Third-Party Services

The application may use the following third-party services:

  • YouTube API: to play video clips showing real-world usage of words in speech. YouTube usage is governed by Google's Privacy Policy
  • Artificial Intelligence Services: to generate explanations and assist with learning

4. Data Storage

Your learning data (words, progress) is stored locally on your device. We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary for app functionality (such as AI requests for explanations).

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data by uninstalling the application from your device
  • Clear your learning progress in the app settings
  • Request information about stored data

7. Children's Privacy

The application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under this age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new version on this page.
